The `hc_light_card` is used to turn on and off a light. You have the option to show or hide the bightness slider.


![light Card Light](/img/cards/hc_light_card/lightcard_light.png#light-mode-only)![light Card Dark](/img/cards/hc_light_card/lightcard_dark.png#dark-mode-only)

## Usage

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="usage" label="Usage" default>
    ```yaml
    type: custom:button-card
    template: hc_light_card
    entity: <your light entity>
    variables:
        enable_slider: True
    ```
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="template" label="Template">

    <CodeBlock language="yaml">{MyComponentSource}</CodeBlock>

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Variables

| Variable | Default | Required | Description|
|----------|---------|----------|------------|
| enable_slider | False | No | Show or hide the brightness slider|
| card_color | var(--color-orange) | No | The color of the card |
